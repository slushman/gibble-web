Opened in 2019, Anise, Modern Vietnamese Eatery is a full-service Vietnamese restaurant where everything is made in house everyday.

The menu at Anise features the popular Vietnamese dishes pho and bun bowls alongside other Asian standards like stir fry and fried rice.

For gluten-free diners, all dishes are clearly marked and many are gluten-free.

For dairy-free diners, many dishes do not appear to include dairy. 

For all allergies, be sure to mention them to your server. Reviewers mention they will help you order safely.

For vegan and vegetarian diners, there are tons of options, like the vegan pho, vegan spring rolls, spicy edamame, and several tofu-based dishes.

Reviewers rave about:
* Gluten-free chicken pho
* Gluten-free spring rolls
* Lemongrass chicken bun
* Rice cakes
