Opened in 2021, Gina’s Kitchen is a gluten, egg, dairy, and peanut-free bakery and bistro. They serve gluten-free and vegan baked goods as well as, vegan and non-vegan gluten-free hot and cold feel good food for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

The bistro menu at Gina’s Kitchen features açaí and yogurt bowls, sandwiches, and salads. The bakery menu features a wide selection of bakery standards.

For diners with food allergies, most dishes should be safe to eat! Ask at the restaurant about your specific allergies.

For vegan and vegetarian diners, many dishes are vegan, like the vegan grilled cheese and salads on the lunch menu, or have a vegan option.

Reviewers rave about:
* Vegan grilled cheese
* Potato soup
* Maple scone
* Cinnamon streusel

Gina’s Kitchen is located on East Alameda Avenue in Aurora, Colorado, just east of Denver.
