They also specialize in allergen friendly baking, including gluten, eggs, dairy, wheat, peanuts, tree nuts, coconut, & yeast.

The menu at Make, Believe Bakery features an oat-based soft serve ice cream, cupcakes, cinnamon rolls, cakes, cookies, and pumpkin bread.

For gluten-free diners, about half the menu is gluten-free and items containing gluten are clearly marked.

For dairy-free and vegan diners, everything is dairy and animal product free.

Ask at the bakery about other food allergies.

Reviewers rave about:
* Oat-based soft serve ice cream
* Brownies
* Homemade peanut butter cups
* Cookie dough
* Cake

Make, Believe Bakery storefront opened in 2016 and is located on 13th Avenue in Capital Hill, just one block south of the capital building.
