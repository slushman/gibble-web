Opened in 2010 in Louisville, Sweet Cow is a bustling neighborhood ice cream shop offering a selection of traditional and creative flavors that change often in each location.

For gluten-free diners, each flavor has a page on their website, which includes ingredients and allergens. Reviewers mention you can also ask at the counter.

For dairy-free and vegan diners, each location has multiple, rotating vegan flavors. Check the website to see which is available at your preferred destination.

Reviewers rave about the flavors:
* Lemon poppyseed
* Root beer float
* Big Lebowski
* Key lime pie
* Fluffernutter
* Cookie dough
* Banana oreo
* Golden Grahams
* Milkshake crunch berry

Sweet Cow has four locations four locations, one on broadway, in uptown on 19th avenue, on Gaylord street in Washington park, and on 25th avenue in Jefferson park.
