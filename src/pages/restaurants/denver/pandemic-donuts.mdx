Pandemic Donuts opened in 2020 after the co-owners both lost their jobs due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

The menu at Pandemic Donuts also features crullers, cinnamon rolls, and, of course, donuts. They change which donut flavors are available each day, so check their website before visiting - or take your chances.

For gluten-free diners, reviewers have mentioned the vegans donuts may also be gluten-free or could be made so.

For dairy-free and vegan diners, Pandemic Donuts offers a vegan donut in the same flavors of the day as their regular donuts.

Reviewers rave about he creative flavors, including:
* Matcha raspberry
* Blueberry lavender
* Key lime pie
* Honey glaze
* Maple nut

Pandemic Donuts is located on Welton Street inside Queen City Collective Coffee in the Five Points neighborhood of Denver.
