Opened in 2020, Crestone Bakery is a dedicated gluten and peanut-free bakery that also offers some dairy-free and other allergen-free baked goods. It is owned and operated by Lori Gottschalk, a Registered Dietitian Nutritionist (RDN), after her husband was diagnosed with Celiac.

Crestone Bakery features a rotating menu, with most items being available daily. 

For gluten-free diners, everything is gluten-free.

For dairy-free and other food allergy diners, there are many items available. The menu clearly marks which allergens are present in each item.

For vegan and vegetarian diners, there are items available, but most bakery items contain eggs. Ask at the bakery to be sure.

Reviewers rave about:
* Cinnamon rolls
* Carrot cake
* Lemon bars
* Sandwich bread

Crestone Bakery is located on North Pecos Street in Broomfield, Colorado, just north of Denver.
