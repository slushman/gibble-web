# Restaurant Queue: Orlando, Florida
_Last updated: 2026-03-11_
_Note: Ethos Vegan Kitchen (closed Sept 2024) was excluded. Dixie Dharma and Valhalla Bakery have left Market on South; the space now features A Lo Vegano and Vegan Scoops._

## Pending

- [ ] ### Earthy Picks
  **Rating:** 4.9/5 on Uber Eats (700+ reviews), 4.9/5 on DoorDash (200+ reviews)
  **Accommodates:** Vegan, Dairy-free
  **Description:** 100% plant-based Colombian-Latin cafe in downtown Orlando near Lake Eola. Vibrant flavors with dishes like chickpea curry, lentil soup, and the New Age Burrito. Opened in 2023 and quickly became a local favorite for its fresh, nourishing ingredients and welcoming atmosphere.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.earthypicks.com/

- [ ] ### Fresh Kitchen
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Yelp (479 reviews, Alafaya location)
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Keto, Paleo, Vegan
  **Description:** 100% gluten-free fast-casual chain with multiple Orlando-area locations. Build-your-own bowls with farm-to-table ingredients, cold-pressed juices, and healthy treats. Dedicated gluten-free facility with antibiotic-free meats. A go-to for celiac diners in Central Florida.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.findmeglutenfree.com/biz/fresh-kitchen/4609979142897664

- [ ] ### Raglan Road Irish Pub
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Tripadvisor, top-rated GF at Disney Springs
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Dairy-free
  **Description:** Authentic Irish pub at Disney Springs where approximately 90% of the menu is gluten-free with a dedicated GF fryer. Manager personally takes celiac orders at the table and reviews menu items. The gluten-free fish and chips get rave reviews. A family of 4 celiacs eats here twice a year.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.findmeglutenfree.com/biz/raglan-road-irish-pub/5246123

- [ ] ### Winter Park Biscuit Company
  **Rating:** 4.6/5 on Restaurant Guru (1,114 reviews), 4.6/5 on Sirved
  **Accommodates:** Vegan, Dairy-free
  **Description:** All-vegan restaurant known as "the best vegan chicken place in Orlando." Biscuits and gravy, sandwiches, burgers, pancakes, waffles, and tenders — all 100% plant-based. Located in East End Market on Corrine Drive.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.winterparkbiscuitco.com


- [ ] ### Bolay
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Tripadvisor, top-rated on FindMeGlutenFree
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Vegan
  **Description:** Florida-born 100% gluten-free fast-casual chain with Chipotle-style build-your-own bowls. All proteins, greens, grains, and sauces are dairy-free (only dairy is in the cookie and parmesan). Multiple Orlando-area locations. Affordable at $8-11 for adult portions.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.findmeglutenfree.com/chains/4584216791351296/bolay

- [ ] ### The Earthy Kitchen
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 (225 reviews)
  **Accommodates:** Vegan, Dairy-free
  **Description:** Orlando's premier plant-based Puerto Rican restaurant, established in 2020. Traditional dishes reimagined for the vegan palate — standout items include vegan chicharron, empanadas, and the chik'n dish with perfect texture. Located in East Orlando.
  **Website/Menu:** https://theearthykitchen.com/

- [ ] ### Erin McKenna's Bakery
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on FindMeGlutenFree, highly rated on Tripadvisor
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Vegan
  **Description:** Dedicated gluten-free, dairy-free, soy-free, and egg-free bakery at Disney Springs. All treats also free of refined sugar. Staff is very knowledgeable about allergens. The Thin Mint Cookie is a fan favorite. A lifesaver for allergy families visiting Disney.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.erinmckennasbakery.com/

- [ ] ### Plantees
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on HappyCow, highly rated across platforms
  **Accommodates:** Vegan, Dairy-free
  **Description:** 100% plant-based fast-food spot in Mills 50 District serving burgers, shoestring fries, nuggets, and shakes. Reviewers call the shakes "the best I've had in any vegan restaurant anywhere on three continents" and the burgers "best vegan burger in Orlando, hands down."
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.happycow.net/reviews/plantees-orlando-287898

- [ ] ### Kosher Grill
  **Rating:** 4.3/5 on Tripadvisor (159 reviews), #546 of 2,799 restaurants in Orlando
  **Accommodates:** Kosher, Halal
  **Description:** Glatt kosher restaurant on International Drive serving Israeli and Middle Eastern cuisine — falafel, shawarma, schnitzel, kuffta kabab, and baklava. Clean, inviting atmosphere with warm service. Open Sun-Thu and Friday before Shabbat.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.koshergrillorlando.com/

- [ ] ### Jerusalem Restaurant
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Tripadvisor, 4.3/5 on Sirved (420 reviews on Yelp)
  **Accommodates:** Halal
  **Description:** Zabihah halal-certified Middle Eastern restaurant in Kissimmee (near Orlando) serving gyros, shawarma, fattoush, kibbeh, and baklava. Reviewers praise the tender lamb and chicken, especially with garlic sauce. Fast service with generous portions.
  **Website/Menu:** https://jerusalemrestaurantorlando.com/

- [ ] ### The Halal Shack
  **Rating:** Well-reviewed on Yelp
  **Accommodates:** Halal, Dairy-free, Nut-free, Gluten-free
  **Description:** Build-your-own halal bowls, salads, fry bowls, hummus bowls, naanritos, and pita wraps. Full allergen menu available — almost everything is dairy-free, no peanuts or tree nuts used, with ample gluten-free and vegan options. A rare multi-allergen-friendly halal spot.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.yelp.com/biz/the-halal-shack-orlando

- [ ] ### White Rabbit Dessert Experience
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Yelp (111 reviews)
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free
  **Description:** Alice in Wonderland-themed dessert restaurant near Waterford Lakes serving indulgent Belgian waffles, cakes, and brownies with a generous gluten-free section. GF waffles get their own dedicated waffle iron. Not a dedicated GF facility — celiacs should verify protocols.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.whiterabbitdessertexperience.com/

- [ ] ### CFS Coffee
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Yelp (245 reviews, Dr. Phillips location)
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Vegan
  **Description:** Allergen-friendly coffee shop with 6+ Orlando-area locations offering GF yucca waffles, smoothie bowls, healthy toasts, and arepa sandwiches alongside specialty coffee. Vegan and dairy-free options throughout the menu. Colombian-inspired with a health-conscious approach.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.cfscoffee.com/

- [ ] ### Nona Street Pizza
  **Rating:** 10/10 from multiple reviewers, highly praised on Yelp (30 reviews)
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free
  **Description:** Neo-Neapolitan pizzeria in Orlando's SODO district offering gluten-free and cauliflower crust pizzas. Staff are careful about cross-contact for GF orders. Reviewers call it "best pizzeria in town hands down." Small, chef-driven operation behind Brewlando.
  **Website/Menu:** https://nonastreetpizza.com/
