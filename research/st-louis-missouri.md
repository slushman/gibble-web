# Restaurant Queue: St. Louis, Missouri
_Last updated: 2026-03-11_

## Pending


- [ ] ### Bombay Food Junkies
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Yelp (272 reviews), best-rated vegan restaurant in Creve Coeur
  **Accommodates:** Vegan, Dairy-free, Gluten-free
  **Description:** 100% vegan Indian fusion restaurant and milkshake bar with zero single-use plastic and 100% compost rate. Many gluten-free options alongside the fully plant-based menu. Customers travel hours to visit, praising the buttered chick'n and vegan cheesecake.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.bombayfoodjunkies.com/

- [ ] ### Olive + Oak
  **Rating:** 4.8/5 on OpenTable (1,676 reviews), #108 of 1,931 restaurants in St. Louis
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Vegan
  **Description:** Upscale American bistro in Webster Groves where a kitchen staff member has celiac disease. Dedicated fryers, most menu items are or can be made gluten-free, and servers are exceptionally knowledgeable about allergens. Regularly offers GF, vegetarian, and vegan options.
  **Website/Menu:** https://oliveandoak.oohosp.com/menu/

- [ ] ### Britt's Bakehouse
  **Rating:** 4.8/5 on Restaurant Guru (366 reviews), 100% recommended on Facebook
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free
  **Description:** 100% dedicated gluten-free bakery in Kirkwood making everything from scratch in small batches. Staff are deeply knowledgeable about celiac disease. Famous for custom cakes and baked goods so good non-GF eaters can't tell. Go early — items sell out.
  **Website/Menu:** https://brittsbakehouse.com/

- [ ] ### Tree House
  **Rating:** 96% recommended on Facebook (1,056 reviews), multiple "Best Vegetarian" awards
  **Accommodates:** Vegan, Dairy-free, Gluten-free
  **Description:** Award-winning plant-based restaurant on South Grand where everything is vegan unless requested vegetarian. Won Best Vegetarian (RFT/Sauce/Alive), Favorite Gluten-Free (Sauce), and Healthiest Restaurant (Fitness Alliance). Many dishes can be made gluten-free.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.treehousestl.com/

- [ ] ### Vegan Deli and Butcher
  **Rating:** 100% recommended on Facebook, "Recommended" by St. Louis Post-Dispatch, Feast 50 Awards Restaurant of the Year 2024
  **Accommodates:** Vegan, Dairy-free
  **Description:** Chef Chris Bertke assembles vegan meats from scratch at this south St. Louis sandwich shop. Runner-up for Best Butcher at the Feast 50 Awards — the first vegan entry ever in that category. Open Wed-Sat 11am-5pm or until sold out.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.stlvegandeliandbutcher.com

- [ ] ### The Golden Hoosier
  **Rating:** 4.6/5 on FindMeGlutenFree (427 reviews on Yelp)
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Dairy-free
  **Description:** Burger joint with both fryers dedicated gluten-free, plus GF bread and bun options. Nearly everything on the menu is or can be made GF. Staff are highly knowledgeable about food allergies and take them seriously. Listed among St. Louis' most celiac-friendly restaurants.
  **Website/Menu:** https://thegoldenhoosier.com/faq

- [ ] ### Songbird
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Yelp (592 reviews), #1 Gluten Free Breakfast in St. Louis on Yelp
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Vegan
  **Description:** Beloved brunch spot in The Grove that makes its own gluten-free bread in-house. Every menu item is annotated for GF and vegan status, and staff are cross-contamination savvy. The GF breakfast sandwich is legendary. Started at Tower Grove Farmers' Market.
  **Website/Menu:** https://songbirdstl.square.site/

- [ ] ### Kalbi Taco Shack
  **Rating:** 4.6/5 on Tripadvisor (#353 of 1,930 restaurants in St. Louis)
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Nut-free
  **Description:** Korean fusion taco restaurant at City Foundry with a totally nut-free kitchen. All marinades are gluten-free and nut-free. Staff follow celiac protocols — changing gloves and preventing cross-contamination. MSG-free with vegetarian and vegan options too.
  **Website/Menu:** https://kalbitacoshack.com/

- [ ] ### Revel Kitchen
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Yelp (217 reviews, Brentwood location)
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Paleo, Keto, Whole 30, Vegan
  **Description:** Fast-casual build-your-own bowl restaurant with locally sourced, organic whole foods. Detailed nutrition guide labeled for every diet. Choose a base (greens or cauliflower rice), add veggies, protein, and sauce. Note: wheat wraps are available, so celiacs should communicate needs.
  **Website/Menu:** https://eatrevelkitchen.com/

- [ ] ### Frida's
  **Rating:** 4.4/5 on Google (313 reviews on Yelp), Best Chef 2024 (St. Louis Post-Dispatch)
  **Accommodates:** Vegan, Dairy-free
  **Description:** Plant-based restaurant in University City with a scratch kitchen free of sugar, HFCS, MSG, butter, and microwaves, using little to no oil. Chef Natasha Kwan was voted Best Chef 2024. Creative dishes like sunflower taco "meat" and chickpea tuna wraps.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.eatatfridas.com/

- [ ] ### Seedz Cafe
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Yelp (304 reviews)
  **Accommodates:** Vegan, Dairy-free
  **Description:** Women-owned, veteran-owned 100% vegan cafe in Clayton known for upscale plant-based dining. Famous for the vegan Reuben with tempeh and cashew chipotle cheddar. Sustainable, organic focus with a warm, inviting atmosphere. Compared favorably to Chicago's top vegan spots.
  **Website/Menu:** https://seedzcafe.com/

- [ ] ### Station No. 3
  **Rating:** Best New Restaurant 2022, St. Louis Magazine Top 100
  **Accommodates:** Vegan, Dairy-free, Gluten-free
  **Description:** Primarily plant-based gastropub by Chef Natasha Kwan serving scratch-made vegan comfort food — burgers, fries, brunch, shakes — that's also welcoming to meat-eaters. Kirkwood location has a drive-through. Flexitarian approach makes it great for mixed dining groups.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.station3stl.com/

- [ ] ### Felix's Pizza Pub
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on FindMeGlutenFree
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free
  **Description:** Pizza pub in Dogtown with 2 dedicated gluten-free pizza ovens and separate prep space. Staff are knowledgeable about allergens — one reviewer noted a bartender flagged gluten in Alfredo sauce without being asked. Not a dedicated GF facility, but strong protocols.
  **Website/Menu:** https://felixspizzapub.com/

- [ ] ### Gokul Indian Restaurant
  **Rating:** 4.0/5 on Yelp (220 reviews)
  **Accommodates:** Kosher, Vegan, Vegetarian
  **Description:** The only 100% vegetarian and kosher restaurant in St. Louis, located in the Delmar Loop. Over 100 menu items with daily all-you-can-eat buffets. Offers a fully vegan dinner buffet every Monday night. Under kosher supervision.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.gokulrestaurant.com/
