# Restaurant Queue: Austin, Texas
_Last updated: 2026-03-11_

## Pending

- [ ] ### Nori
  **Rating:** 4.8/5 on Google, 4.9/5 on Uber Eats (900+ reviews)
  **Accommodates:** Vegan, Gluten-free, Dairy-free
  **Description:** Fully plant-based Japanese restaurant with creative vegan sushi, ramen, and small plates. The general manager has celiac disease, so the kitchen knows how to prepare Japanese food safely for gluten-free diners. Extensive gluten-free menu alongside the all-vegan commitment.
  **Website/Menu:** https://noriaustin.com/

- [ ] ### ATX Cocina
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Yelp (789 reviews), 5/5 on FindMeGlutenFree
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Dairy-free
  **Description:** 100% gluten-free and seed-oil-free upscale Mexican restaurant built around masa. One of the head chefs has celiac. Reviewers say you'd never know the entire menu is gluten-free — it's just a genuinely great restaurant.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.atxcocina.com/


- [ ] ### The Well
  **Rating:** 4.6/5 on Restaurant Guru (1,893 reviews), 4.8/5 on OpenTable (Westlake)
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Keto, Paleo, Whole 30
  **Description:** Dedicated gluten-free restaurant with 3 Austin locations. Entire menu is free of gluten, dairy, seed oils, soy, refined sugar, and GMOs. Celiac diners can order anything worry-free. Clean, sustainably sourced ingredients throughout.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.eatwellatx.com/

- [ ] ### Picnik
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Yelp (917 reviews)
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Nut-free, Paleo, Keto, Whole 30, Vegan
  **Description:** 100% gluten-free kitchen that also eliminates corn, soy, and peanuts. Detailed allergen guide for every dish, and staff calls to verify allergy needs on pickup orders. Known for butter coffee, grain-free pancakes, and paleo-friendly comfort food.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.picnikrestaurants.com/

- [ ] ### Bistro Vonish
  **Rating:** 4.9/5 on Tripadvisor (190+ reviews on Yelp)
  **Accommodates:** Vegan, Dairy-free
  **Description:** Popular all-vegan food truck in North Loop serving elevated modern cuisine with local Central Texas produce. The Spicy Brussels Mac and Cheese and cinnamon rolls are standout items. Frequently cited as the best vegan food truck in Austin.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.bistrovonish.com/

- [ ] ### Bulevar Mexican Kitchen
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on OpenTable (1,525 reviews)
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Dairy-free
  **Description:** 100% gluten-free Mexican kitchen using King Arthur's gluten-free flour. One of the head chefs has celiac disease and is dedicated to safe gluten-free dining. Latin American-inspired cuisine in a modern, upscale setting.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.findmeglutenfree.com/biz/bulevar-mexican-kitchen/6279365359435776

- [ ] ### Casa de Luz
  **Rating:** 4.4/5 on Tripadvisor (#170 of 2,422 Austin restaurants)
  **Accommodates:** Vegan, Gluten-free, Dairy-free
  **Description:** Nonprofit community center serving a daily-changing, fixed-price macrobiotic meal that is 100% organic, vegan, and gluten-free. Communal dining with a serene atmosphere near Zilker Park. Meals under $15.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.casadeluz.org/

- [ ] ### Honest Mary's
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Tripadvisor and Yelp (607+ reviews)
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Paleo, Whole 30, Vegan, Keto
  **Description:** Fast-casual grain bowl restaurant with an all-scratch kitchen. Customizable bowls for any dietary need — Whole30, paleo, gluten-free, vegan. Organic, locally sourced, non-GMO oils, no refined sugar. Multiple Austin locations.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.honestmarys.com/

- [ ] ### Odd Duck
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Yelp (2,430 reviews)
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Dairy-free
  **Description:** Celebrated farm-to-table small plates restaurant where servers ask about celiac and adjust the menu accordingly. Not a dedicated GF facility, but the whole-ingredient focus means many dishes are naturally gluten-free with knowledgeable allergen-aware staff.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.oddduckaustin.com/

- [ ] ### TLC Austin
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on OpenTable
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Nut-free, Dairy-free
  **Description:** 99% gluten-free menu with dedicated gluten-free fryer. No soy in the kitchen, and nuts appear in only a few items. Famous for gluten-free chicken fried steak, hush puppies, and fried okra that taste like the real deal.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.findmeglutenfree.com/biz/tlc-austin/6455746401271808

- [ ] ### Zucchini Kill Bakery
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Yelp (161 reviews)
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Vegan, Dairy-free
  **Description:** Dedicated gluten-free, vegan, and soy-free bakery with a punk rock aesthetic. Musician-owned feminist bakery serving cupcakes, cookies, coffin cakes, and seasonal Swiss rolls. Fully dedicated facility makes it one of Austin's safest options for celiacs.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.zucchinikill.com/

- [ ] ### Rebel Cheese
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Yelp (353 reviews), 10,000+ 5-star reviews online
  **Accommodates:** Vegan, Dairy-free
  **Description:** Award-winning artisanal vegan cheese shop and bistro featured on Shark Tank. House-made aged brie, cheddar, blue cheese, camembert, and more. Four of five Shark Tank sharks loved the products. A must-visit for dairy-free diners.
  **Website/Menu:** https://rebelcheese.com/

- [ ] ### Lick Honest Ice Creams
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Yelp (1,465 reviews)
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Vegan
  **Description:** Local ice cream chain with house-made coconut milk vegan flavors and an all-gluten-free base. Clear allergen labeling on every flavor. Only one year-round flavor contains nuts. Vegan gluten-free ice cream sandwiches available.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.ilikelick.com/

- [ ] ### The Halal Corner
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Yelp (517 reviews), 92% recommended on Facebook
  **Accommodates:** Halal
  **Description:** Popular halal restaurant in Mueller serving shawarma, gyro, falafel, hummus, and kafta kebab. Generous portions at reasonable prices (most items under $10). Known for consistent, flavorful food and quick service.
  **Website/Menu:** https://www.thehalalcorner.com/

- [ ] ### Maaribu Cafe + Gifts
  **Rating:** 4.5/5 on Yelp (38 reviews)
  **Accommodates:** Gluten-free, Dairy-free
  **Description:** Dedicated gluten-free bakery and cafe owned by someone with celiac disease. Scratch-made organic cupcakes, cookies, scones, and specialty lattes. Unique concept combining a home decor showroom with a health-conscious bakery.
  **Website/Menu:** https://maaribu.com/
